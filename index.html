About

The Social Intelligence Lab investigates the cognitive mechanisms that underlie our ability to adapt successfully to our social environment. Furthermore, we investigate intentional and cognitive control of behavior.

We are hiring!

Dear colleagues and friends, We are seeking a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to join the social intelligence lab at the Berlin School of Mind and Brain, Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin. This unique six-year position offers an exceptional opportunity to advance research and teaching in the field of social neuroscience using VR and neuroimaging techniques. We offer a highly international, team-oriented and dynamic research environment in the heart of Berlin with state of the art neuroimaging, motion tracking and VR facilities. Ideal candidates will have: - Completed a scientific university degree and PhD in cognitive psychology/cognitive neurosciences or a related field - Experience in experimental cognitive neuroscience research (especially EEG) - Very good knowledge of statistics and programming skills (e. g. Python, Matlab) - Relevant publication experience in international journals - Very good written and spoken English (at least B2) - Basic German language skills - High ability for independent work and high social competence - Desirable: experience with virtual reality (e. g. in Unity) and with motion capture Please send your application (including a cover letter, CV, relevant certificates and contact information of two referees) referencing the job IDAN/202/25 to Prof. Dr. Marcel Brass, Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, Faculty of Life Sciences, Department of Psychology by e-mail (additional with ’postdoc social intelligence’ in the subject line) in a single PDF file to jurena.wille@hu-berlin.de by the end of July.
